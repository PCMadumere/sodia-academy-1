About SODIA

SODIA serves as a rural-to-global digital talent pipeline, equipping young Nigerians with practical digital skills, certifications, and career opportunities needed to thrive in the global digital economy. The Academy combines practical training, industry-aligned learning, hands-on labs, and measurable employment outcomes to prepare students for opportunities in technology and innovation.
